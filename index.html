A Little Something
In the Garden —— 花园里
This morning I walked into the garden with my tea. The tomatoes are still green, but the mint has gone wild. I picked a few leaves and put them in my cup. The sun was warm on my shoulders, and a small orange butterfly landed on the basil. I stood still and watched. It stayed for a few seconds, then flew away.
In the corner, the sunflowers are taller than me now. They follow the sun all day. I watered them slowly, one by one. The soil smelled like rain from last night. I thought: this is a good kind of slow.
✦ ✦ ✦
今早端着茶走进花园。番茄还是青的，但薄荷已经疯长了一地。摘了几片放进杯子。阳光暖暖地落在肩上，一只橙色的小蝴蝶停在罗勒上。我站着没动，看着它。它停了几秒，然后飞走了。
角落里的向日葵已经比我高了。它们一整天都追着太阳转。我一棵棵慢慢地浇水。泥土里有昨夜雨水的味道。我想：这种慢，真好。
A Little Something
A Quiet Thing —— 一件安静的事
Yesterday afternoon it rained. Not hard — just a soft rain that made everything quiet. I sat by the window with my second cup of tea. No phone, no music. Just the sound of rain on the leaves.
I remembered something my grandmother used to say: "Rain is nature's way of telling us to slow down." She was right. I didn't do anything important that afternoon. And it was perfect.
✦ ✦ ✦
昨天下午下雨了。不大——只是一场让一切都安静下来的小雨。我坐在窗边喝着第二杯茶。没有手机，没有音乐。只有雨打叶子的声音。
我想起外婆以前说过的一句话："雨是大自然在告诉我们，慢一点。"她说得对。那个下午我什么重要的事都没做。但特别完美。
Try saying this out loud: "It was a quiet afternoon, and I enjoyed every minute of it."
试着轻轻念出来：那是一个安静的下午，我享受了每一分钟。
Words I Love
三个有画面感的词 —— words that paint pictures
bloom
/bluːm/ 动词 · 开花，绽放
"The roses are blooming in the garden."
花园里的玫瑰正在盛开。
"She bloomed into a confident woman."
她绽放成了一个自信的女人。这个词可以用来形容花，也可以用来形容人。
cozy
/ˈkoʊ.zi/ 形容词 · 舒服的、温暖的、让人安心的
"I lit a candle and made my corner of the sofa cozy."
我点了一根蜡烛，把沙发角弄得暖暖的。cozy 不只是一个词，是一种生活态度。
wander
/ˈwɑːn.dɚ/ 动词 · 漫步，漫无目的地走
"After lunch, I wandered around the neighborhood."
午饭后，我在附近漫无目的地走了一圈。wander 和"散步"的不同，在于它没有目的地。
Try This, If You Feel Like It
☕ 一件小事
"Find one thing in your home that is green. Look at it for ten seconds. Then say one sentence about it in English."
在家里找一件绿色的东西。看它十秒钟。然后用英文说一句话描述它。

比如你看到阳台上的一盆绿萝，可以说：

"This plant has big, beautiful leaves. It makes the room feel alive."

说给自己听就好。窗台上的花不会评判你。

五月的主题是绿色。试试之后你会发现：原来自己已经能说一句完整的英文了。
